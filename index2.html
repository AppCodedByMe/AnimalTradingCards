Zebra

The pattern of a zebras stripes are different for each individual zebra, making them each as unique snowflakes!

  • Scientific Name: Equus quagga
  • Average Height: 5 ft.
  • Average Lifespan: 20 - 30 years
  • Habitat: Treeless Grasslands and Savanna Woodlands

The black & white striped pattern of their coats is a good bug repellant, keeping horseflies and other blood-suckers at bay. A group of zebras is called a "zeal."
